THE FOUNDER

Legal training, applied to sourcing.

Meridian International was founded by George Skordi, a dual-qualified legal professional (LLB, LLM) with operational experience across property investment and international business management. George is resident in Guangzhou, China, with direct access to the Pearl River Delta manufacturing corridor — the world's most concentrated manufacturing region.

The founder's legal training defines the operational character of the practice. Every document Meridian produces — engagement letters, RFQ briefs, supplier assessment reports, trade compliance advisories — is drafted to the standard expected of a legally trained professional. This is the foundation of Meridian's distinction within the sourcing and trade facilitation sector.

Meridian is a Hong Kong-registered agency (Meridian Capital Holdings Limited, BR No. 76904892-001-08-25-5) operating on a commission and fee basis. Meridian acts as agent on behalf of its clients and holds no proprietary interest in the goods it sources.

Within Meridian's role

  • Sourcing and trade facilitation, acting as agent on the client's behalf
  • Physical presence in Guangzhou, with direct Pearl River Delta factory access
  • Legally trained document preparation, applied to every engagement
  • A Hong Kong-registered entity, invoicing in USD and HKD

Outside Meridian's role

  • Meridian is not a trading company, and does not buy, hold, or resell goods
  • Meridian is not a freight forwarder or customs broker; such services are referred to licensed third parties
  • Meridian is not a law firm; legal literacy is applied to commercial practice, and does not constitute regulated legal advice
  • Meridian is not a certification body; product testing and certification is coordinated with accredited laboratories
