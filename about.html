THE PRACTICE

George Skordi | Principal

LLB  ·  LLM  ·  Principal

Meridian International was established to address a specific gap in the market for professional sourcing and procurement advisory services between China and international buyers.

Businesses engaging with China for procurement face a market that is structurally difficult to navigate from the outside. China's internal commercial systems, supplier networks, verification processes, and trade documentation requirements operate differently from those in Western markets. Managing these without professional support from within that environment carries real risk — in supplier quality, contract terms, compliance exposure, and cost.

The options available to most businesses are limited. Large sourcing companies serve volume clients. Freight forwarders manage logistics, not procurement decisions. Informal contacts operate without professional accountability. The result is that businesses either absorb the complexity themselves or proceed without adequate advisory support.

Meridian provides structured professional advisory services covering the full sourcing process — from initial supplier identification through to contract review, trade compliance, and inspection coordination. The practice operates from Hong Kong with access to Pearl River Delta supplier networks and direct familiarity with how procurement and manufacturing operate within China's commercial environment.

Every engagement is handled personally by the principal. Clients deal directly with the practice — not with a junior team or account management layer.

The practice is at an early stage of development. Meridian does not claim a transaction history it does not have. What it offers is a professionally structured advisory service, documented methodology, and a principal with the qualifications and background to manage a China sourcing mandate with precision and accountability.

SCOPE OF ENGAGEMENT

What the practice provides

  • Sourcing and procurement advisory, acting in a representative capacity on the client's behalf
  • Access to Pearl River Delta supplier networks and familiarity with China's commercial and regulatory environment
  • Professional document preparation and written advisory notes on every engagement
  • A Hong Kong-registered practice invoicing in USD and HKD
  • Direct principal accountability — every engagement is handled by George Skordi personally

What the practice does not provide

  • Meridian does not buy, hold, or resell goods
  • Meridian is not a freight forwarder or licensed customs broker; logistics and customs filing are referred to licensed third parties
  • Meridian is not a law firm; legal training is applied to commercial practice and does not constitute regulated legal advice
  • Meridian is not a certification body; product testing and certification is coordinated with accredited laboratories
  • In-person factory visits and on-site inspections are coordinated through accredited third-party partners; personal site attendance by the principal is available where warranted and agreed
