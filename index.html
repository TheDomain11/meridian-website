WHY MERIDIAN

Pearl River Delta access

Meridian operates from Hong Kong with direct access to the Pearl River Delta manufacturing corridor. Supplier identification, verification, and assessment draw on established networks and direct familiarity with how manufacturing and procurement operate within China's internal systems.

Professionally structured

Every document Meridian produces — supplier assessments, RFQ briefs, contract reviews, compliance advisories, engagement letters — is prepared to a professional standard. The practice is led by a legally trained professional holding both an LLB and LLM.

Representative, not principal

Meridian does not buy, hold, or trade goods. The practice acts in a representative and advisory capacity on behalf of its clients. The commercial relationship is between client and supplier. Meridian manages that relationship on the client's behalf.

SERVICES

01

Supplier identification and verification

Research, registration verification, and scored assessment of manufacturers using the Meridian Supplier Assessment Protocol. Delivered as a documented shortlist with a primary recommendation and stated reasoning.

02

RFQ management and negotiation

Structured preparation, distribution, and management of requests for quotation. All quotes compared on a like-for-like basis. Recommendation and negotiation record delivered in writing.

03

Contract drafting and review

Drafting and review of the supply agreement between client and supplier — covering price, quantity, quality standards, delivery obligations, and remedies for breach. Drafted with legal precision.

04

Trade compliance and documentation

HS classification, import duty advisory, applicable trade agreement analysis, and commercial document preparation — covering South Africa, the United Kingdom, and the European Union.

05

Quality inspection coordination

Briefing and management of pre-shipment inspections through QIMA, Bureau Veritas, SGS, or Intertek. Written advisory on the accept, reject, or remediate decision issued within 24 hours of the inspection report.

06

Sourcing consultation

A structured advisory session for businesses evaluating a China sourcing mandate. Findings and recommended next steps delivered in writing within 24 hours.
