THE PROBLEM

Sourcing from China should not require a leap of faith.

Most importers operate without visibility. They select a supplier from a marketplace listing, transfer funds to an unverified account, and hope the product delivered matches the photographs. When it does not — wrong specifications, concealed defects, delayed shipments, unresponsive suppliers — they are thousands of kilometres away with limited recourse. Meridian International exists to eliminate that uncertainty.

Suppliers who appear identical online

The majority of marketplace listings belong to trading companies presenting as manufacturers. Without ground-level verification, the distinction is impossible to make from abroad.

Samples that do not reflect production output

Pre-order samples are frequently hand-finished to specification. Production runs are not. Without in-person quality control prior to shipment, discrepancies surface only after goods have arrived.

Payment transfers with no contractual protection

Transferring funds to an unverified overseas account is among the most common ways importers incur unrecoverable losses. Without a structured contract and a known counterparty, recourse is limited.

Logistics failures at the border

Incorrect HS codes, missing certificates of origin, and incomplete customs documentation are predictable and preventable. Without expertise at the documentation stage, they become costly delays.

THE PROCESS

From initial brief to your warehouse, in five stages.

01

Brief & Verify

You provide your product specification and target parameters. Within 48–72 hours, you receive a comparison of three to five verified suppliers with pricing, MOQ, lead time, and a documented recommendation.

02

Sample & Negotiate

Samples are ordered to our Guangzhou address and inspected in person against your specification. Preferred suppliers are then engaged in structured price and terms negotiations on your behalf.

03

Order & Oversee

The order is placed through Meridian International — our Hong Kong entity. Production is monitored throughout, with regular progress updates and immediate escalation if timelines or quality are at risk.

04

Inspect & Ship

A pre-shipment QC inspection is conducted at the factory using AQL sampling standards. Shipment is authorised only after you approve the inspection report. Freight, customs documentation, and tracking are managed through to delivery.

05

Reconcile & Repeat

You receive a final landed cost reconciliation and a project debrief. Repeat orders benefit from all prior verification work — subsequent orders typically complete in significantly less time than the first.

Every project follows this structure. Variations are communicated at the outset, not discovered in transit.
