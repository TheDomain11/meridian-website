PRICING

Pricing

Meridian's fees are structured and transparent. Commission is calculated on the FOB supplier invoice value. Standalone fees apply where a specific service is required without a full engagement. Third-party costs are invoiced at cost in every instance; Meridian applies no markup.

COMMISSION STRUCTURE

Commission on FOB value.

Commission is calculated on the confirmed FOB (Free On Board) supplier invoice value, agreed in the engagement letter before work begins. The minimum engagement fee applies to all mandates. On smaller transactions, where the calculated commission falls below USD 1,500, the minimum engagement fee applies in place of the percentage.
