FEE STRUCTURE

Fee structure.

Meridian's fees are structured and transparent. Indicative rates for each service are set out below; the applicable fee for a specific engagement is confirmed in writing in the engagement letter before any work commences. Clients with an existing verified supplier relationship may qualify for a reduced scope — and reduced fee — on repeat verification, documentation, or RFQ engagements, since foundational research is already complete.

FULL MANDATE ENGAGEMENTS

For full engagement mandates covering the complete sourcing process, Meridian's fee is calculated as a percentage of the confirmed FOB supplier invoice value, agreed in the engagement letter prior to commencement.

Transaction value (FOB)Commission rate
Under USD 25,0008 – 10%
USD 25,000 – 100,0005 – 8%
USD 100,000 – 500,0003 – 5%
Over USD 500,000Negotiated

A minimum engagement fee of USD 1,500 (HKD 11,700) applies to all full mandates. Where the calculated commission is below this amount, the minimum engagement fee applies in its place; where commission exceeds it, only the commission is invoiced.

THIRD-PARTY COSTS

Inspection fees, laboratory certification charges, customs brokerage fees, and freight costs are always invoiced separately at cost. Meridian applies no markup. Supporting documentation is attached to every pass-through line item.

Meridian invoices in USD and HKD. All HKD amounts are calculated at the Hong Kong dollar peg rate of HKD 7.80 = USD 1.00.
