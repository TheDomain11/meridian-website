FEE STRUCTURE

Fee structure.

Meridian's fees are structured and transparent. The applicable fee for each service or engagement mandate is agreed in writing and set out in the engagement letter before any work commences.

Fees are quoted on request following an initial consultation. The structure below sets out the basis on which fees are calculated.

ADVISORY SERVICES

Each advisory service carries a fixed fee based on estimated professional time and the scope of the specific engagement. Fees for standalone services are quoted on request.

FULL MANDATE ENGAGEMENTS

For full engagement mandates covering the complete sourcing process, Meridian's fee is calculated as a percentage of the confirmed FOB supplier invoice value. The applicable rate is agreed in the engagement letter prior to commencement. A minimum engagement fee applies to all full mandates.

THIRD-PARTY COSTS

Inspection fees, laboratory certification charges, customs brokerage fees, and freight costs are always invoiced separately at cost. Meridian applies no markup. Supporting documentation is attached to every pass-through line item.

Meridian invoices in USD and HKD. All HKD amounts are calculated at the Hong Kong dollar peg rate of HKD 7.80 = USD 1.00.
