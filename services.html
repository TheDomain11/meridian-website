SERVICES

Sourcing, quality control, logistics, and advisory — priced transparently.

Four service tiers, structured to accommodate different requirements and scales of engagement. Most clients begin with sourcing and expand the scope as the relationship develops. All work is conducted through Meridian International — our Hong Kong entity — with formal contracts, documented invoicing, and clear accountability at every stage.

Sourcing & Procurement

8–12% commission on FOB value
or 15–25% margin on buy-resell

The foundation of every engagement. We identify and verify suppliers, negotiate terms, and manage the order from confirmation through to the factory gate — with full documentation at each stage.

What is included

  • Supplier identification and verification — factory audits, business licence checks, and production capacity assessment
  • Sample sourcing and in-person evaluation prior to any recommendation
  • Price negotiation and commercial contract preparation
  • Order placement and production monitoring with regular progress updates
  • Pre-shipment quality control inspection at basic level, included as standard
When to select this service: You have a product specification but no verified supplier, or you have had a negative experience with a marketplace supplier and require a more structured approach.

Quality Assurance

US$200–350 per inspection day
or bundled with sourcing

Standalone quality control for clients who source independently but require in-country inspection. Alternatively, layered onto sourcing engagements for high-value or specification-sensitive products.

What is included

  • In-line production inspections — identifying defects before they propagate through the production run
  • Pre-shipment inspection using AQL sampling in accordance with ISO 2859
  • Factory compliance audits covering social, environmental, and safety standards
  • Defect documentation with full photo and video reporting delivered within 24 hours
  • Independent verification — our inspector works exclusively for the buyer, not the supplier
When to select this service: You have already selected a supplier but require independent eyes on the ground, or the order value is sufficient that a defective shipment would represent a materially significant loss.

Logistics Coordination

Bundled with sourcing
or US$150–300 per standalone shipment

End-to-end coordination of the freight and documentation process — from the factory gate to your warehouse — ensuring compliance, accuracy, and no avoidable delays at customs.

What is included

  • Freight forwarder selection, briefing, and ongoing management
  • Export documentation preparation — commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading
  • Customs compliance documentation — certificates of origin, test reports, HS code verification
  • Amazon FBA preparation coordination where required
  • Shipment tracking and delivery confirmation reporting
When to select this service: You are managing your own sourcing but logistics are absorbing disproportionate time, or you have encountered customs complications on previous shipments.

Advisory Services

US$100–200 per hour
or project-based retainers

Structured advisory for engagements that fall outside the standard sourcing model. Drawing on a legal background and sustained on-the-ground experience in China's manufacturing sector.

What is included

  • Commercial contract review and terms negotiation advisory
  • Intellectual property protection — trademark registration and design patents in China
  • Supplier dispute resolution and structured mediation
  • Market entry consulting for businesses expanding into China sourcing for the first time
  • Strategic supplier diversification planning and risk assessment
When to select this service: Your situation requires more than transactional sourcing — a supplier dispute, a contract requiring review, or a strategic decision about how your sourcing function should be structured.

Why we publish our pricing.

Most sourcing agents present fees only after a prospect has engaged. We regard that practice as a friction point rather than a negotiating tool. The rates above reflect what we charge. Final fees vary with order complexity and volume, and a specific figure is always provided before any work commences. There are no undisclosed margins embedded in supplier pricing, no supplementary fees added post-engagement, and no commission arrangements with suppliers — the pricing you are quoted is the pricing you pay.

Not certain which service applies to your situation?

Provide a brief description of the product you are sourcing or the problem you are working through. We will respond with a direct recommendation — even if that recommendation is that a different approach would serve you better.
