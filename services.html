SERVICES

Six services.

Meridian provides six advisory services, available on a standalone fee basis or as components of a full engagement mandate. Every engagement is governed by a signed engagement letter. No work commences without one. Fees are quoted on request following an initial consultation.

Service 01

Supplier identification and verification

WHAT MERIDIAN DOES

Meridian conducts structured research into manufacturers and trading companies across Alibaba, Made-in-China, Global Sources, and established Pearl River Delta supplier networks. Every shortlisted supplier is verified through China's National Enterprise Credit Information Publicity System (NECIPS), confirming business registration, registered operating scope, export licence status, and credential consistency.

Where the engagement requires physical verification, factory visits and on-site assessments are coordinated through accredited third-party partners operating within China.

DELIVERABLES

  • Meridian Supplier Longlist — all identified candidates with source documentation and suitability assessment
  • Meridian Supplier Shortlist Report — verified candidates scored using the Meridian Supplier Assessment Protocol, with a primary recommendation and stated reasoning
  • Meridian Factory Visit Report — where a visit is coordinated: structured observations, photographic record, and assessment against the client's specification

SCOPE

Formal third-party audits (SA8000, ISO, BSCI) fall outside Meridian's scope. Where required, these are coordinated with SGS, Bureau Veritas, or Intertek and invoiced to the client at cost.

MERIDIAN IP

The Meridian Supplier Assessment Protocol is a proprietary five-category scoring methodology. The Supplier Longlist, Shortlist Report, and Factory Visit Report templates are copyright protected.

Service 02

RFQ management and negotiation

WHAT MERIDIAN DOES

Meridian prepares a structured RFQ based on the client's complete product specification and distributes it to the verified supplier shortlist. All supplier responses and clarifications are managed by Meridian. Negotiation covers unit price, minimum order quantity, payment terms, lead time, and sample arrangements. Every quote is evaluated on a like-for-like basis — consistent Incoterm, consistent MOQ — before a recommendation is issued.

DELIVERABLES

  • Meridian RFQ Document — formally drafted prior to distribution, with commercial terms stated explicitly including Incoterm basis, payment terms, and liability allocation
  • Meridian Quote Comparison Report — tabular comparison of all quotes received, negotiation record, and a written recommendation with reasoning specific to the terms quoted

MERIDIAN IP

The Meridian RFQ Template and Quote Comparison Template are copyright protected.

Service 03

Supply Agreement Coordination & Documentation Support

WHAT MERIDIAN DOES

We help structure and document your supply arrangement with your chosen supplier to the commercial terms you instruct — covering price, quantity, quality standards, delivery obligations and remedies. We prepare working documentation reflecting your instructions in our capacity as your commercial representative. This is not legal advice. We recommend your final agreement be reviewed by independently instructed qualified counsel before signature.

DELIVERABLES

  • Meridian Agreement Structuring Note — a structured or reviewed supply agreement with each clause's commercial effect and risk allocation set out in plain terms
  • Written summary of recommended amendments with supporting rationale, provided prior to execution

SCOPE

This service constitutes contract coordination and review support provided by an unregulated advisory practice. It does not constitute regulated legal advice. For high-value or complex transactions, or where a dispute is anticipated, Meridian recommends engaging qualified legal counsel in the relevant jurisdiction.

MERIDIAN IP

The Meridian Supply Contract Template is copyright protected.

Service 04

Trade Documentation Support & General Tariff Information

WHAT MERIDIAN DOES

General information on HS classification, indicative import-duty ranges, and applicable trade-agreement provisions for South Africa, the United Kingdom, and the European Union, plus preparation of commercial documents (proforma invoices, commercial invoices, packing lists). This is general informational support, not regulated customs, tax, or legal advice. Meridian does not act as your customs broker or declarant of record. Customs declarations must be lodged by an agent licensed or established in the destination jurisdiction — we can help you identify and engage one.

DELIVERABLES

  • Meridian Trade Compliance Advisory Note — HS classification with rationale, applicable duty rates, trade agreement provision applied, and estimated landed duty cost
  • Certification Requirements Summary — mandatory and voluntary certifications by destination market, with recommended accredited laboratories
  • Commercial document set — commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin advisory, and bill of lading review note

SCOPE

Meridian provides HS classification advisory and documentation support. Formal customs declarations and filing with SARS, HMRC, or EU customs authorities require a licensed customs broker. Meridian refers to a licensed broker and does not file on the client's behalf.

MERIDIAN IP

The Trade Compliance Advisory Note Template, Commercial Invoice Template, and Packing List Template are copyright protected.

Service 05

Quality inspection coordination

WHAT MERIDIAN DOES

Meridian establishes the client's quality requirements, prepares a structured inspection brief, and engages an accredited third-party inspection company — QIMA, Bureau Veritas, SGS, or Intertek. On receipt of the inspection report, Meridian reviews findings against the client's specification and issues a written advisory within 24 hours stating clearly whether to accept, reject, or request supplier remediation.

DELIVERABLES

  • Meridian Inspection Brief — submitted to the inspection company prior to the inspection date
  • Meridian Inspection Advisory Note — findings summary and recommendation, issued within 24 hours of the report
  • Full third-party inspection report forwarded to the client in parallel

SCOPE

Inspections are performed by the engaged accredited third party. Third-party inspection costs are invoiced to the client at cost.

MERIDIAN IP

The Meridian Inspection Brief Template is copyright protected.

Service 06

Procurement consultation

WHAT MERIDIAN DOES

A structured commercial advisory session for businesses evaluating a China sourcing mandate. Findings and recommended next steps delivered in writing within 24 hours. This is commercial and procurement consultancy, not legal advice.

DELIVERABLES

  • Structured one-hour advisory session with agenda provided in advance
  • Meridian Consultation Summary Note — issued within 24 hours, recording the advice given and recommended next steps

FEE NOTE

The consultation fee is applied as a credit toward Meridian's fee on proceeding to a full engagement mandate.
